# Customers

## Credit Score

Every customer starts with a credit score of **100**. Updated automatically based on payment behavior.

| Event | Score Change |
|---|---|
| On-time paluwagan payment | +1 |
| On-time layaway payment | +1 |
| Late paluwagan payment | -2 |
| Late layaway payment | -3 |

**Badge tiers** (shown on customer card):
- **Excellent:** 90–100
- **Good:** 70–89
- **Fair:** 50–69
- **Poor:** < 50
